Bears 🐻

Here at our zoo, we have one type of bear: Sun Bears. Sun Bears are known for spending more time in trees than other bears and for sunbathing.

Giraffes 🦒

Giraffes are the tallest mammals on Earth and are known for their long necks, spotted patterns, and long legs. They are herbivores and primarily eat leaves, fruits, and flowers.

  • Coconut

Lions 🦁

Lions are the only cats that live in groups, which are called prides. Our African lions may remind you of The Lion King, but don't go near them!
  • Mella
  • Karl

Monkeys 🐒

Monkeys are known for their similarity to humans in intelligence and features. Our woolly monkeys spend a lot of time up high, so be sure to check unexpected places to spot them.

Alligators 🐊

Alligators are reptiles found in swamps and marshes (only occassionally indoors in a Florida household). Adult male alligators, such as Wren and Aspen, are on average 10-15 feet long.
